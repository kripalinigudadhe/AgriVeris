Privacy Policy

Data Collection

AgriVeris collects personal information necessary for verification, including farm data, crop information, and transaction history. All data is encrypted and secured using industry-standard protocols.

AI Model Usage

Our AI models analyze your data to verify authenticity and sustainability claims. Data used for model training is anonymized and aggregated. You have the right to opt-out of optional data sharing.

Blockchain Recording

Verification results are recorded on the blockchain as an immutable record. This data includes crop type, verification status, and batch information. Personal information is hashed for privacy.

User Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. You can request a data export at any time. Contact our privacy team for any concerns regarding your data.
