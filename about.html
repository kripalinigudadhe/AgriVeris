About AgriVeris

Building Trust Through Technology

Our Vision

A world where agricultural transparency is paramount. Where every farmer is empowered to prove their commitment to sustainability, every buyer can trust the origin of their food, and fake claims have nowhere to hide. We envision an agricultural ecosystem built on trust, technology, and fairness.

Our Mission

To leverage AI and blockchain technology to verify the authenticity of farm produce and sustainability claims, reward genuine farmers for eco-friendly practices, and create a transparent marketplace where consumers can make informed decisions about what they consume.

How It Works

Step 1: Data Collection

Farmers submit production data, soil samples, crop images, and IoT sensor readings

Step 2: AI Analysis

Machine learning models verify authenticity, quality, and sustainability metrics

Step 3: Verification

Independent verifiers audit results and confirm authenticity through consensus

Step 4: Blockchain Recording

Immutable verification records are stored on the blockchain for transparency

Step 5: Marketplace

Verified products are listed on the marketplace for buyers to purchase

Step 6: Rewards

Farmers earn rewards in cryptocurrency for verified eco-friendly practices
